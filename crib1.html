I made these sheets publicly available and they were very well received by both students and course providers.

The files were created in Microsoft Word. Many people were surprised to discover that I had used Word and suggested using Latex instead. I am familiar with Latex but since Word is so ubiquitous and familiar to many more people I thought it would be interesting to see how far I could get formatting such technical information. I was pleasantly surprised to discover it is actually quite powerful.
